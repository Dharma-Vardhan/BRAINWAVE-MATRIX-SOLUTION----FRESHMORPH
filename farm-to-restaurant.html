Farm-to-Restaurant Connection

Our Farm-to-Restaurant initiative connects local farmers directly with restaurants, ensuring fresh and organic produce reaches your table. By eliminating middlemen, we support farmers and provide restaurants with the best quality ingredients.

How it works:

  • Farmers list their produce on our platform.
  • Restaurants browse and order directly from farmers.
  • Fresh produce is delivered to restaurants within hours of harvest.

Benefits:

  • Restaurants get the freshest ingredients for their dishes.
  • Farmers earn fair prices for their produce.
  • Reduced carbon footprint due to shorter supply chains.
